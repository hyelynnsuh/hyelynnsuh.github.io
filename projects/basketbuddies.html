designing a completely remote and collaborative grocery shopping experience

I started my freshman year of college during the COVID-19 pandemic, and due to the poor quality of the dining halls and my limited student budget, buying take out was not always my best option. I found that there were not many completely remote options to grocery shop, especially for groups of people (ie. my roommates). I started this project to find the best medium to do so.

the problem:

why doesn't collaborative grocery shopping work online?

understanding user needs

Because I wanted to understand more about the issues of collaborative grocery shopping, I planned a round of 4 user interviews to why most users prefer to go grocery shopping alone and in person.

click on the cards to see what i found

need for live group collaboration
the current market of online grocery delivery services do not give users features that promote collaboration.
narrowing down different app use
online grocery services currently require the use of too many other apps for group orders.
saving time is optimal
users spend anywhere between 1-2 hours on grocery shopping – both planning and physically shopping.
